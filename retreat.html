HCRC/CLASS 2016 Summer Writing Retreat

edited 5.4.16

The writing retreat will be held at Twin Larch Retreat and Conference Center from Monday, May 23rd through Friday, May 27th, 2016. The writing retreat is sponsored by the Human Community Research Consortium and College of Letters, Arts, and Social Sciences at the University of Idaho. The tentative retreat schedule and driving directions to Twin Larch can be found below.

Participants are encouraged to attend the entire writing retreat, but daily participation is not required. Please RSVP for the writing retreat by emailing Stacey Camp. In the email, please provide a brief summary of your writing project (1-3 sentences), what you want to accomplish during the retreat, and what days/times you can attend. Note that a specific writing project is not required to attend the retreat. If you do not have a specific writing project in mind, the writing retreat is still a wonderful time and space to consider new writing and research projects.

For those of you new to Twin Larch, note that internet service is limited.

*NOTE: Breakfast and lunch will not be provided on these days. Please pack your own beverages, snacks, and anything else you might need to do good writing.
